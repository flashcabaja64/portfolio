Mood Journal

Live GitHub

This mood journal is a documentation of your moods and/or emotions. We can go through a wide-spectrum of different emotions, but without the self-awareness to look deeper into their thought process, we can sometimes feel out-of-touch with ourselves. One of the goals of this application is to help users discover hidden meanings to their psyche. Every entry will go through a data collection process and in turn reveal patterns to their moods and/or emotions.

Learn Spanish Med-Terms

Live GitHub

Get a simple start with learning Spanish Medical Terminology by way of spaced repetition. Users can first navigate to 'Study Mode' where they can study the spanish terms and english translations. Once they feel comfortable and want to prove their translation ability, they can switch over to 'Quiz Mode' and test their skills. Each word will keep track of correct, incorrect, and total score to ensure the terms are properly memorized.

Chore Runner

Live GitHub

If keeping track and assigning chores to your precious children are difficult, turn it into a game by using Chore Runner. Each household, a parent can assign chores for their children that rewards points after a child logs into their own account to approve them. To add more spice to the game, children can compete with their fellow siblings to see who has collected the most points from their completed chores in the leaderboards page. What better way to teach your children discpline and responsibility than turning a tedious and boring task into a interesting one.
